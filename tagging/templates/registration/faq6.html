I forgot my username/password. How can I retrieve it?

In case you wish to retrieve your username, please enter your master email-id (the email id used for registration with iTechTalents.com) in the “Master Email” text box and click on “Send Username” button. We’ll email you your account details.

If you wish to retrieve your password, please enter your user name in the “Username” text box and click on “Send Password” button. We’ll email you your account details.

If you do not remember either your username or the email id with which you had registered for a iTechTalents recruiter account, please get in touch with us at bugs@iTechTalents.com . Alternatively, please contact us at any of our branch offices.
